Multimodal UAV Simulation Benchmark

We designed and implemented the complete multimodal simulation and data-generation pipeline for a 3D UAV perception benchmark built on the UE4-based CARLA engine. This pipeline integrates realistic UAV flight dynamics, synchronized multi-sensor acquisition, cross-modal timestamp alignment, and unified coordinate transformation, enabling large-scale generation of geometrically consistent RGB, LiDAR, radar, and DVS data across diverse scenes and weather conditions.
